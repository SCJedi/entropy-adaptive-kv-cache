# F-02: Addition and Subtraction

Welcome back! Last time you learned what numbers really are. Now we're going to learn how to DO things with numbers: combining them (addition) and splitting them apart (subtraction). These are tools you'll use constantly in everyday life.

## Addition: Combining Groups

Addition means putting groups together to find out how many you have total.

Let's say you have 3 apples in one bag and 2 apples in another bag. How many apples do you have altogether?

```
Bag 1:  * * *           (3 apples)
Bag 2:  * *             (2 apples)
        в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җ
Total:  * * * * *       (5 apples)
```

We write this as: 3 + 2 = 5

The plus sign (+) means "combine these" or "put these together."
The equals sign (=) means "gives you" or "results in."

Read it out loud: "Three plus two equals five."

## Subtraction: Taking Away

Subtraction means removing some from a group to find out what's left.

You have 5 cookies. You eat 2 cookies. How many are left?

```
Start:    * * * * *     (5 cookies)
Eat:      * *           (2 cookies gone)
          в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җ
Left:     * * *         (3 cookies)
```

We write this as: 5 - 2 = 3

The minus sign (-) means "take away" or "remove."

Read it out loud: "Five minus two equals three."

## Real-World Examples

**Shopping**: You have $20. You buy a book for $12. How much money do you have left?
- Start with: $20
- Subtract: $12
- Left with: $8

**Cooking**: A recipe needs 3 cups of flour. You already added 1 cup. How much more do you need?
- Need: 3 cups
- Already have: 1 cup
- Still need: 3 - 1 = 2 cups

**Time**: You work 5 hours on Monday and 3 hours on Tuesday. How many hours total?
- Monday: 5 hours
- Tuesday: 3 hours
- Total: 5 + 3 = 8 hours

## Some Useful Patterns

**Adding zero**: Any number plus zero equals the same number.
- 5 + 0 = 5
- 100 + 0 = 100
- Zero means "nothing," so adding nothing doesn't change the amount.

**Subtracting zero**: Same thing. Taking away nothing leaves you with what you started with.
- 5 - 0 = 5

**Subtracting from itself**: Any number minus itself equals zero.
- 7 - 7 = 0
- If you take away everything, you have nothing left.

**Order matters for subtraction**:
- 5 - 3 = 2
- 3 - 5 = ? (We'll learn about this in lesson F-04 when we talk about negative numbers!)

**Order doesn't matter for addition**:
- 3 + 5 = 8
- 5 + 3 = 8
- Either way, you end up with 8.
